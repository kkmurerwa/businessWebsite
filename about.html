About Goldberg and Mercer

What makes us who we are?

Our birth

Goldberg and Mercer was created by Harvard Law graduates and senior partners Michael Goldberg and Todd Mercer. The two envisioned an all-in-one law firm, where no client was turned away because they required legal services beyond the scope of the founders' areas of specialization. Together with a few friends, each with their own field of specialization, Goldberg and Mercer was born in 2002. The firm has only gotten bigger and stronger over the years, and has served thousands of clients in its period of existence.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide the best legal service available to the clients and there are several measures we have put in place to ensure this.

  • We only employ attorneys with impeccable education credentials from the best law schools in the country.
  • Senior parners have to have had a clean track record with evidence of dedication to their clients despite the outcome of the cases they have previously handled.
  • Our legal teams work their level best to ensure that all cases are concluded fast and justly.

    • Community policy

    Goldberg and Mercer believes in giving back to the community. It is for this reason that the firm undertakes in several pro-bono cases a year for destitute families in dire need of legal representation. The firm puts a team to the task and ensures a favourable outcome for the party. Contact us to find out how to apply for pro-bono representation.

    Our goal

    Our goal is to become the best legal outfit in the country and to offer quality legal guidance in regard to our core values. The satisfaction of all our clients, even in hopeless cases, is our biggest objective.
