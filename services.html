Our Services

Services you can bet your life on

Goldberg and Mercer is a firm with very diverse talents. The firm has over ten divisions, each with a particular field of law that it majors in. This way, you do not have to worry at all with whatever legal issue you have.

Criminal Law

The firm's biggest area of specialization is criminal law. These is your goto division in case you need legal advise or representation in a trial involving a criminal offence. This may include and is not limited to misdemeanors, petty theft, injury suit, accidents and violations of the vehicle code in your jurisdiction. We also offer strong defense and representation for people involved in felony crimes. Our criminal law team are the best in the field and will ensure a favourable ruling that will leave you satisfied with our services.

Tort Law

Sometimes you may be in need of a lawyer who knows his stuff in order to get settlement or damages for an incident that happened to you because of someone else's negligence or lack of care. Look no further than Goldberg and Mercer. Our tort lawyers are experienced in every trick in the book and whether or not you suffered actual harm in the incident, we will work hard to get you that settlement cheque you are entitled to.

Patent and Copyright Law

Patenting and suing for violations of your patent can be full of pitfals for anyone holding the intellectual property rights to an invention or creation. Given the ever-increasing piracy cases in the country, you need a lawyer who can adequately defend you in case of a copyright violation and help you file for a patent with no worries. And that is exactly what Goldberg and Mercer is here for. We deal in all forms of patenting and copyright infringement so you have nothing to worry about.

Business Law

Whether you are negotiating a deal, or seeking a merger with another company or just seek to sign a contract with another company, Goldberg and Mercer is here to help. With a combination of legal and finance experts, we will not only negotiate a deal without loopholes for you and your company but also make sure that the deal is in your best interest and will have a positive financial outcome.
